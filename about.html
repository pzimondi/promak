About PROMAK

A small company with a big sense of place.

We're not a fleet. We're not a franchise. We're a tight team that loves this part of the world and treats every booking like it's the first one we've ever taken.

Our Story

Built on a simple idea: travel should feel personal.

PROMAK Travel and Tours was founded by Jarsone Makondo, a Cape Town local who spent years driving for the big tourism brands and slowly realised something: the bigger the operation, the colder the experience. The same drives, the same scripts, the same packed-out coaches.

So we started small, on purpose. One vehicle. One driver-guide who actually knows your name by lunchtime. One promise — that your trip feels like it was put together by a friend who happens to know everyone in the industry, because that's exactly what's happening.

We've grown since, but the rule hasn't changed. Every booking gets a real human. Every itinerary gets thought through. Every guest leaves with a story.
